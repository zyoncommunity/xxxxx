Discord Fast Food is a role play for ordering food. All orders are processed through our web panel then delivered by hand through Discord! <br>

Useful Commands
!help - Displays list of commands.
!ping - View the bots api latency.
!order - Order some food for our chefs to make.
!myorder - View your order status.
!feedback - Send us some feedback.
!tip - Send us a tip.
!discord - Invite to our guild.
!invite - Invite for our bot.
